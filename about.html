About UpNews

We are an info-media app which works to help inform and provide the latest data during the coronavirus pandemic. This app provides all information regarding to the pandemic through articles from top news sources to latest easy to read data from the CDC and WHO. UP-NEWS seeks to spread the truth and spread facts to all.

We compile the latest news articles relating to COVID-19 from the CDC and WHO. Demonstrate top Graphs and Statistics relating to Covid-19 from the CDC and WHO. Moreover, we have developed a recommending system using natural language processing to ease user usage. Our safe and secure website allows people to read accurate and relevent information, compiled from the most reliable sources.

Background

The UpNews team has extensive experience in coding, developing, and providing accurate news to the public. Our team works very hard to help fulfill user needs. if you have any questions about our organization, please contact us.

Teamwork

Working together is key, especially during the COVID-19 period. We strive everyday to provide a relevent service to the community, so please work with us, with others, and with the community during these unprecedented times.

Our Core Value

As mentioned before, our core value is providing the community a reliable news source that compiles accurate news and COVID-19 statistics from providers across the globe. We must stay together in the fight against COVID-19, and being informed during these times is key.
